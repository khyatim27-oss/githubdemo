What Are Natural Disasters?

Natural disasters are unexpected events caused by nature that can cause damage to life and property. Examples include Earthquakes, Floods, Cyclones, Wildfires and Volcanic eruptions.

Learning about natural disasters helps us prepare and respond effectively.
